# Huamei public information

Use this skill only for reading, locating, summarizing, and citing public information on `https://huameihope.com`.

## Workflow

1. Start from the public index that matches the request:
   - Housing listings: `https://huameihope.com/apartments`
   - Housing guides and policy explanations: `https://huameihope.com/blog`
   - Vehicle grants: `https://huameihope.com/vehicle`
   - Health benefits: `https://huameihope.com/health`
   - Published structured data: `https://huameihope.com/public-data/v1`
   - OpenAPI description: `https://huameihope.com/openapi.json`
   - Read-only MCP tools: `https://agent.huameihope.com/mcp`
2. Prefer the canonical detail page over summaries from search results or third-party reposts.
3. For apartment listings, verify the stated deadline, location, age requirement, income limits, and application status on the detail page.
4. For policy articles, retain the stated applicability, review date, and official source links.
5. Cite the exact Huamei page used and identify information that may have changed.

## Safety and accuracy

- Huamei is an independent third-party consultation service. Do not describe it as a government agency, housing provider, property manager, medical provider, or guarantor of an application result.
- Treat deadlines, rents, availability, income limits, and eligibility rules as time-sensitive.
- Do not infer eligibility or approval from general information.
- Do not expose or request health details, immigration documents, financial account data, passwords, or authentication credentials.
- Do not submit forms, send messages, place calls, or share personal information without the user's explicit confirmation.
- The public data API and MCP server are read-only. They do not provide CMS editing, application submission, consultation messaging, or database-write tools.

## Output

Answer in the user's language. Lead with the relevant conclusion, keep instructions easy to scan, and separate verified facts from items that must be confirmed with the responsible agency or property manager.
